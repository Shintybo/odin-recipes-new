Ingredients

Now lets get down to the good stuff and see what we will need !

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 Large Onion, Chopped
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed
  • 1 Zucchini, Grated
  • 500g | 1kg Extra lean minced beef
  • 1 Tablespoon tomato paste
  • 80ml Red Wine (Optional)
  • 1 x 700g | 24oz bottle Passata Cooking Sauce
  • 2 Teaspoons vegtable stock powder (or salt to season - adjust to your tastes
  • 2 Teaspoons dried oregano
  • 3 Tablespoons low fat cream for cooking (or low fat milk
  • Handful of freshly chopped parsley
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese, to serve
  • 1 x 500g | 1kg packet spaghetti
  • Salt for the pasta cooking water

Now we have all this awesomness collected.......Lets cook this storm up!

Step by step guide

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan; add the onions, garlic, carrots and zucchini. Fry until vegetables are soft and fragrant (about 3 minutes). Add the beef and fry until browned. Add the tomato paste; pour in the wine and boil until it has reduced and thickened. Reduce heat and stir in the Passata, vegetable stock powder (or salt), and oregano.
  2. Cover pan with a lid and simmer until the sauce has thickened, stirring occasionally (about 20-30 minutes). Turn off heat; add the cooking cream (or milk) and stir until combined.
  3. Cook the spaghetti in a pot of boiling salted water. Drain; add 2 ladlefuls of the sauce and mix until pasta is covered. Divide between 6 serving dishes; top with extra sauce and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and parsley
